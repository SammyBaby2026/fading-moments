A Faded Family Album

I found this album in a cardboard box at the back of my grandmother's closet. The pages stick together now, and some of the faces I no longer recognize. But the moments remain—captured in silver halide, waiting to be remembered.

This small exhibition presents a selection from that album. Not the whole story—just fragments, edges, the spaces between what was said and what was felt.
