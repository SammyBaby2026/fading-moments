Winter Coat

Putting on a heavy coat after months of lighter layers. The weight of it feels like an embrace I didn't know I needed. The pockets still hold last year's ticket stubs, a crumpled tissue, loose change. Small artifacts of a previous winter, waiting to be rediscovered.
