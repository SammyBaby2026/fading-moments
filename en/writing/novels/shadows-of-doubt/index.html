About This Novel

Ashford, a dying industrial city in America's Rust Belt. Fifteen years ago, the Harrison Steel Mill exploded, killing twenty-three night-shift workers. The official ruling: tragic accident. The truth: far more sinister.

Now, the survivors are dying—one by one—in "accidents" that bear the signature of a meticulous killer. Each victim is found with a rusted gear in their hand, marked with the number 15.

Detective Jack Morrison, the only survivor who walked away from that night, must unravel a conspiracy that reaches into the highest levels of Ashford's power structure. But the deeper he digs, the more he realizes that the killer—known only as The Machinist—knows secrets about Jack's own past that could destroy him.
