Shadows of Doubt

Ashford, Pennsylvania — A dying steel town in America's Rust Belt. Fifteen years ago, the Harrison Steel Mill exploded, killing twenty-three night-shift workers. Now, a killer known as The Machinist is hunting the survivors one by one. Detective Jack Morrison (38) was the only survivor who walked away from that night. He must catch a killer who knows his secrets before the clock runs out. But the deeper he digs, the more he realizes that some doors, once opened, can never be closed. With investigative journalist Sarah Chen (34) — whose father died in the explosion — as his only ally, Jack must confront the past he's been running from for fifteen years.
